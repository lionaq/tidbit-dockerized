Community Guidelines

To ensure a positive and enjoyable experience for everyone, please take a moment to familiarize yourself with these rules.

  1. Respect: Treat all members with kindness and respect. Remember that we share a passion for food and cooking.
  2. No Harmful Content: Do not post or share content that is offensive, harmful, or discriminatory. This includes avoiding hate speech, bullying, or any form of harassment.
  3. Authenticity: Share your own recipes, creations, and experiences. Plagiarism is not tolerated.
  4. Safety First : Be mindful of food safety practices and share recipes responsibly. Avoid promoting dangerous or harmful cooking methods.
  5. Privacy: Respect the privacy of others. Do not share personal information without consent.
  6. Content Ownership : By posting on our platform, you grant us the right to use and display your content. Ensure you have the necessary permissions for any content you share.
  7. Feedback: Provide constructive feedback and reviews. Help fellow users improve their culinary skills.
  8. Reporting: If you encounter inappropriate content or behavior, report it to our moderation team.
